Instruction:

To make the plugin work.
1. Copy the qml files to plugins directory.
2. Open musescore and create a new score with the key signatures D major or Bb.
3. Begin your composition.
3. Now go to plugins >plugin manager > reload plugin > check mark Bb and D# plugins.
4. To add solfa notation to specific measure, select the notes then goto >>plugins > notes > Bb or D# based on your key signature.
5. To add solfa to entire composition, select all by pressing CMD+A or CRTL+A, plugins>>notes>>Bb or D#.

*****Caution*****
Do not apply this plugin more than once else it will result in duplicate solfege.
